PSL July 4 Cookout

For years, the City of Pacifica has hosted a day of fun in the sun on the Fourth of July. This year, we serve up lunch.

Menu

This year's menu includes pulled pork sandwiches, veggie chilli with toppings, burgers, and hot dogs. For sides we have watermelon, coleslaw, and nachos.

About Us

The Pacifica Sea Lions swim team was founded in 1964 and is a non-profit competitive swim team. We are constantly growing and training young swimmers, building confidence and a sense of community.
